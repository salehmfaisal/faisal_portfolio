About

Hi! My name is Abu Saleh Mosa Faisal. I am a graduate student of Applied Mathematics (Operations Research and Statistics). I am passionate on Data Science. My academic and professional expertise is in Business and Biology Data Analytics.

Hello, I am currently studying as a M.S. Student at the Department of Mathematics, New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology. I have previously obtained my M.S. and B.S. in Applied Statistics from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. The focus of my research is in developing and applying machine learning algorithms to high-dimensonal genomics data, business data and public health data. Previously, I have worked in a bank as Data Analyst. I dealt with business, sales, HRA data to produce useful insights for management. During my M.S., I worked as a Graduate Student Researcher (GSR) and Graduate Teaching Assistant (GTA) where I have gained extensive experience in working with collaborators from a wide range of research background.
